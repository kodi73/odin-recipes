Samosa

Description

Samosa is an highly enjoyed, tasty Indian delicacy which is incredibly easy to make. It can be found in most of states of India. It is made up of potato stuffing.

Ingredients

For the dough (outer pastry shell): All-purpose flour (maida) – 2 cups, Carom seeds (ajwain) – ½ tsp (optional, for aroma and digestion), Salt – ½ tsp, Oil or ghee – 4 tbsp (for shortening, makes the pastry flaky), Water – as needed (to knead a stiff dough)

For the stuffing (spiced potato filling): Potatoes – 3 large, boiled and mashed, Green peas – ½ cup (optional, boiled or steamed) , Green chili – 1 finely chopped (optional, for heat) , Ginger – 1 tsp grated , Cumin seeds – 1 tsp , Coriander powder – 1 tsp , Cumin powder – ½ tsp , Garam masala – ½ tsp , Red chili powder – ½ tsp (adjust to taste) , Amchur (dry mango powder) – ½ tsp (for tanginess) , Salt – to taste , Fresh coriander leaves – 2 tbsp chopped , Oil – 1 tbsp (for tempering spices)

For frying: Oil – enough for deep-frying (~2–3 cups depending on pan size)
